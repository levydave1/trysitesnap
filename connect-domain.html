Connect Your Domain

Follow these simple steps to bring your site live.

1

Copy these values

A Record (Host: @) 76.76.21.21
Click to Copy
CNAME Record (Host: www) cname.vercel-dns.com
Click to Copy
2

Where to paste them?

GoDaddy Instructions ▼
1. Log in to GoDaddy Portfolio.
2. Click DNS next to your domain.
3. Find the A Record with name @ and change its value to 76.76.21.21.
4. Find CNAME with name www and set it to cname.vercel-dns.com.
Namecheap Instructions ▼
1. Log in and go to Domain List.
2. Click Manage -> Advanced DNS.
3. Add/Edit A Record: Host @, Value 76.76.21.21.
4. Add/Edit CNAME: Host www, Value cname.vercel-dns.com.
Any Other Provider ▼
Look for "DNS Settings", "Zone Editor" or "Manage DNS". You just need to update the A Record and CNAME using the values from Step 1.

Common Questions

How long does it take?

DNS changes usually take 10-30 minutes, but can take up to 24 hours to reach everyone worldwide. Be patient!

Will my email stop working?

No. As long as you only touch the "A" and "CNAME" records and leave the "MX" records alone, your email (Outlook/Gmail) will work perfectly.

How do I know it's working?

You can check your status live at DNSChecker.org. Enter your domain and look for the IP 76.76.21.21.
